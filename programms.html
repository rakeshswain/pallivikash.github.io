Health Awareness Campaign

We promoted regular health awareness campaign at target facilitating Senior Citizen, Women, Children with health check up and free medicine support. Women Empowerment and Livelihood Management

In the target area we have formed and managed women self help groups, federations for setting up of their employment cum income generating opportunity facilitating their livelihood restoration management through firm and non-frim activities. We supported the groups with skill and capacity building, training, value addition of produces, marketing and buy-back arrangement raw materials and products, exposure visit and pricing and products. Further women groups of the locality have been properly guided by us on micro credit, micro insurance gender justice, institutional delivery, small family norms, women atrocity, women trafficking.

Literacy Awareness Campaign & Library Services

Rural Development

Our organization since inception have promoted participatory and contributory method for development through Shramadan (Labour contribution) for maintenance of rural roads, caring of water bodies, setting up of cottage Industries with local area raw materials and technical know how.

Youth Welfare Activities

To identify leadership talents among youths we arranged youth leadership training regularly facilitating local area youths from different communities to a common platform to sharing their experience for all-round socio economic development and employment cum income opportunity. Further the youth participants were motivated to keep up national integration, peace in the locality.
