Our Major Causes

The main aim and objective of Pallivikash is enhanchment of Socio Economic Condition of poor/tribal people of our socity.

Health Awareness Campaign

We promoted regular health awareness campaign at target facilitating Senior Citizen, Women, Children with health check up and free medicine support. In the campaign the participants are being motivated and explained regarding water sanitation, RCH & Small Family Norms, Pulse Polio, Malaria, Cancer HIV AIDs, Institutional delivery etc.

Women Empowerment and Livelihood Management

In the target area we have formed and managed women self help groups, federations for setting up of their employment cum income generating opportunity facilitating their livelihood restoration management through firm and non-frim activities. We supported the groups with skill and capacity building, training, value addition of produces, marketing and buy-back arrangement raw materials and products.

Raised: $50,689 Total Need: $500k

Environmental protection & development

Our organization is always with the target area people motivating them for conservation and preservation of local area forest, mangro forest, and wild life. Through regular campaigns in the area all our activities have been possible for protection and development of degraded forests with kind cooperation of the tribal people. Further we have developed nursery garden having a huge stock of fuel, fodder and medicinal rare herbs. We have undertaken house hold solid waste management activities in urban slum areas’.

Raised: $50,689 Total Need: $500k

Rural Development

Our organization since inception have promoted participatory and contributary method for development through Shramadan (Labour contribution) for maintenance of rural roads, caring of water bodies, setting up of cottage Industries with local area raw materials and technical know how.

Raised: $50,689 Total Need: $500k

Youth Welfare Activities
